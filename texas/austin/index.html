Austin Home Project FAQs

How much do home renovations cost in Austin?

Most Austin-area renovations range from $5,000–$80,000+ depending on scope. Bathrooms often run $9,000–$30,000, kitchens $15,000–$50,000+, and roofing $7,000–$18,000 for typical homes.

What home projects add the most value?

In the Austin market, kitchen remodels, roof replacement after storm damage, and energy-efficient HVAC commonly deliver strong resale appeal.

What permits are common in Austin?

Full roof replacements, HVAC changeouts, electrical panel upgrades for solar, and many structural remodels require city or county permits in the DFW metro.

Are solar panels worth it in Texas?

Yes for many homeowners with bills above $150/month and good sun exposure. Texas offers strong solar production and the 30% federal tax credit. See our solar calculator.

What roofing materials work best in Austin?

Architectural asphalt shingles are most common. Impact-resistant (Class 4) shingles are popular after hail. Metal roofing is growing in suburban new builds.
