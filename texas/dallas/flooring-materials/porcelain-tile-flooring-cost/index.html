Porcelain Tile Pros & Cons

Advantages

  • Extremely durable
  • Waterproof when grouted/sealed
  • Ideal for kitchens & baths
  • Works with radiant heat

Disadvantages

  • Higher labor cost than LVP
  • Hard underfoot — area rugs help
  • Grout maintenance required

Waterproof Performance

Porcelain tile is one of the best waterproof flooring options when installed with proper membrane and grout care.

  • Kitchens: Spill and splash resistant
  • Bathrooms: Standard for showers and floors
  • Entryways: Mud and rain tolerance
  • Laundry: Appliance leak protection

Porcelain Tile FAQ

How much does porcelain tile flooring cost?

Porcelain tile typically costs $8–$12 per sq ft installed.

Is porcelain tile waterproof?

The tile body is waterproof; grout joints need sealing for full moisture protection.

Tile vs LVP in kitchen?

Tile wins on durability and heat; LVP wins on cost and softer feel.
