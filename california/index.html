California Home Project FAQs

How much do home renovations cost in California?

Most California renovations range from $8,000–$120,000+ depending on metro. Bathrooms often run $12,000–$38,000, kitchens $22,000–$75,000+, and roofing $9,000–$22,000 with coastal labor premiums.

What roofing material works best in California?

Composition and tile are common. Coastal wind ratings and wildfire ember zones influence material and vent choices.

Are solar panels worth it in California?

Often yes with strong sun and time-of-use rates. See our solar calculator and the 30% federal tax credit.

How does Title 24 affect HVAC and solar?

California energy rules favor high-efficiency equipment on many replacements — factor compliance into bids.

What projects add the most home value in California?

Solar, kitchen remodels, and energy-efficient HVAC commonly appeal to buyers in coastal metros.
