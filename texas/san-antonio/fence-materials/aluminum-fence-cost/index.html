Aluminum Fence Pros & Cons

Advantages

  • Will not rust like steel
  • Elegant ornamental appearance
  • Pool and HOA friendly
  • Minimal maintenance

Disadvantages

  • Less privacy than wood or vinyl panels
  • Higher cost than chain link
  • Dents possible from impacts

Best Uses for Aluminum Fencing

Aluminum is ideal when you need decorative boundaries and code compliance without wood upkeep.

  • Front yards — ornamental street appeal
  • Pool enclosures — self-closing, self-latching gates
  • HOA communities — consistent black or bronze palette
  • Coastal homes — salt air resistant powder coat

    Aluminum Fence FAQ

    How much does an aluminum fence cost?

    Aluminum fencing typically costs $30–$50 per linear foot installed.

    Is aluminum better than wrought iron?

    Aluminum is lighter and rust-free; wrought iron is heavier and may need refinishing.

    Does aluminum work for pool fences?

    Yes — pool-rated aluminum systems with self-closing gates are widely used for code compliance.
